Hello I am

FATEMEH NOORI

As a biomedical engineer, I am deeply passionate about understanding the mechanisms of the human body and finding technological solutions for medical challenges. My goal is to bridge the gap between the world of engineering and direct clinical applications. My focus is on how we can utilize advanced tools to study cells and tissues at small scales. I am particularly interested in how the physical and mechanical properties of cells can aid us in the early diagnosis of diseases. I have hands-on experience with imaging techniques and biosensors, and I am eager to deepen my expertise in fields such as Tissue Engineering and Biomaterials. I believe the future of medicine lies in this creative intersection.
